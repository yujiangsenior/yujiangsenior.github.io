About Me

I am currently a PhD candidate (expect to graduate 2025.11) at University College Cork, where my research focuses on developing learned simulators to optimize industrial processes through machine learning and reduced-order models.

Previously, I served as a Research/IT Assistant at Westlake University, CenBRAIN, where I supported advanced research projects and managed IT infrastructure from January 2019 to March 2021. I also worked as a Quantitative Analyst in the futures research industry in 2018, applying machine learning algorithms for market prediction, and as a piping design engineer in Hangzhou for 3 years, where I contributed to system designs and optimization.

I hold a BE from Zhejiang University (2011) and an ME from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (2014).
