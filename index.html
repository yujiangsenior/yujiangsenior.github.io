About Me

I am currently a PhD student at University College Cork, focusing on developing learned simulators aimed at optimizing industrial processes.

My previous experience includes working as a Research/IT assistant at Westlake University, CenBRAIN, from January 2019 to March 2021. Prior to that, I worked as a quantitative analyst in a futures research organization in the year 2018 and spent 3 years as a piping engineer.

I graduated in 2011 with a BE from Zhejiang University and in 2014 with a ME from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.
